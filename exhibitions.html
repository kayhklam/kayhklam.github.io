Portraits For NHS Heroes

#PortraitsForNHSHeroes was created by Tom Croft on Instagram, an initiative to honour the heroes of 2020 pandemic and I was inspired to take part in this project whilst in lockdown. Some were on the frontline COVID-19 response and it has shone a positive light in these challenging times, a way to show my gesture of appreciation and thanks to honour our frontline heroes.

Portraits For NHS Heroes online exhibition by Google Arts & Culture

Fine Art book publication Portraits For NHS Heroes by Tom Croft, Bloomsbury Publishing released 12th November 2020



Hoi Polloi

Degree Show Exhibition Richard Hamilton Building, Oxford Brookes University, Oxford (May 2018)

Fusion (2018) 140cm x 100cm, Giclée laminate matte print on Foamex board

This self-portrait sets two distinctive culinary styles against each other, exploring the conflictions of cultural duality. Baked beans in a Chinese bowl, English Breakfast in a Chinese teapot, a fried egg eaten over rice with chopsticks.

Selected permanent artwork for Student Art Collection at Oxford Brookes University.
