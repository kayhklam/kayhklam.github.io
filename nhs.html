Portraits For NHS Heroes

#PortraitsForNHSHeroes was created by Tom Croft on Instagram, an initiative to honour the heroes of 2020 pandemic and I was inspired to take part in this project whilst in lockdown. Some were on the frontlines of COVID-19 response and gifting portraits to the recipients has shone a positive light in these challenging times, a way to show my gesture of appreciation and thanks to honour our frontline heroes.
