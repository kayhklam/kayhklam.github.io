 <h1> Degree Show work</h1>

  <h2><i>Fusion</i>, 2018</h2>

  <p>140cm x 100cm</p>
  <p>Giclée laminate matte print on Foamex board</p>
  <p>This self-portrait sets two distinctive culinary styles against each other, creating a battleground where two
    cultures fight for dominance. Baked beans in a Chinese bowl, English Breakfast in a Chinese teapot, a fried egg
    eaten over rice with chopsticks. This is an analogous of the conflict of the experiences from my identity of
    cultural duality.</p>
  <p>Permanent artwork for Student Art Collection at Oxford Brookes University</p>

  <img src="image/FUSION_Kay Lam.jpg" />

  <p>Installation at the <i>Hoi Polloi</i> Degree Show 2018 at Oxford Brookes University</p>

  <img src="Image/Installation Fusion.jpg" /><br> 

  <h2><i>Where Do You Really Come From?</i>, 2018</h2>

  <p>30cm x 50cm</p>
  <p>Digital paintings, 1 min loop videos </p>
  <p>A series of digitally painted self-portraits that takes a playful approach to tackling the issue of ethnic
    stereotyping, editing my own facial features to assimilate my identity into the very stereotypes others impose upon
    myself through their assumptions of my ethnicity.
    Chinese, Tibetan, Korean and Japanese.</p>

  <img src="image/Self-portraits ethnicity.jpg" />

  <p>Merging each identity in a fluid, mirrored and off-sync manner.</p>
  <p>Video installations at the <i>Hoi Polloi</i> Degree Show 2018 at Oxford Brookes University</p>

  <img src="Image/Installation self-portraits ethnicity.jpg" /><br>
