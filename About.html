Info

Kay Lam is a British-Chinese visual artist, born and based in Essex, UK. She works in digital and traditional paintings, graphic design and digital motion.

After receiving a BA in Fine Art at the School of Arts, her Fusion photograph was selected as a permanent artwork for Oxford Brookes University's Student Art Collection of 2018.
