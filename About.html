Kay Lam is a British-born Chinese multidisplinary artist/ illustrator based in Essex UK.

After receiving a BA in Fine Art at the School of Arts, her 'Fusion' photograph was selected as a permanent artwork for Oxford Brookes University's 'Student Art Collection' of 2018.

She works in various media including digital and traditional paintings, design and animation.


Available for commissions, collaborations and partnerships. To get in touch, please contact kaylamartist@gmail.com
