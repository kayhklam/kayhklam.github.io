Kay Lam is a freelance illustrator based in Essex, specialising in 2D digital and traditional painting.

A fine art graduate from Oxford Brookes University, Kay's degree show 'Fusion' photograph was nominated and selected as a permanent artwork for the Student Art Collection of 2018.
