Kay Lam is a British-born Chinese artist based in Essex UK, specialising in 2D digital and traditional paintings.

Kay is a fine art graduate from Oxford Brookes University who undertook a multidisciplinary art practice during her study, particularly in photography. Her 'Fusion' photograph was selected as a permanent artwork for Oxford Brookes University 'Student Art Collection' of 2018.

For commissions, collaborations and other enquires, please contact kaylamartist@gmail.com
