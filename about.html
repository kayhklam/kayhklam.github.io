Kay Lam is a British-born Chinese multidisciplinary Artist/ Illustrator based in Essex, UK.

After receiving a BA in Fine Art at the School of Arts, her 'Fusion' photograph was selected as a permanent artwork for Oxford Brookes University's 'Student Art Collection' of 2018.

She works in various media including digital and traditional paintings, design and motion.


Available for commissions and collaborations. To get in touch please contact kaylamartist@gmail.com
