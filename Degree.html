Fusion

140cm x 100cm

Giclée laminate matte print on Foamex board, 2018

This self-portrait sets two distinctive culinary styles against each other, creating a battleground where two cultures fight for dominance. Baked beans in a Chinese bowl, English Breakfast in a Chinese teapot, a fried egg eaten over rice with chopsticks. This is an analogous of the conflict of the experiences from my identity of cultural duality.

Permanent artwork for Student Art Collection at Oxford Brookes University

Installation at the Hoi Polloi Fine Art degree show 2018 at Oxford Brookes University

Where Do You Really Come From?

30cm x 50cm

Digital paintings, 1 min loop videos, 2018

A series of digitally painted self-portraits that takes a playful approach to tackling the issue of ethnic stereotyping, editing my own facial features to assimilate my identity into the very stereotypes others impose upon myself through their assumptions of my ethnicity. Chinese, Tibetan, Korean and Japanese.

Merging each identity in a fluid, mirrored and off-sync manner.

Video installations at the Hoi Polloi Fine Art degree show 2018 at Oxford Brookes University
